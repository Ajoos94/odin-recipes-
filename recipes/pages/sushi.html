Sushi Recipe

  1. 4 ounces sashimi-grade yellowfin tuna, cut into small chunks
  2. ⅓ cup mayonnaise
  3. 3 tablespoons chile oil, or more to taste
  4. 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  5. 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce
  6. 1 green onion, diced
  7. 3 sheets nori, cut in half
  8. ½ small ripe avocado, thinly sliced
  9. ¼ English cucumber, cut into matchsticks
